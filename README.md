# IBM-HR-Dashboard

## Overview
This project provides a comprehensive HR dashboard for IBM, offering insights into various employee demographics and job-related metrics. The dashboard visualizes data related to employee age, gender, hourly rate, job roles, education fields, departments, marital status, and business travel frequency.

## Features
- **Employees by Age**: Distribution of employees across different age groups.
- **Employees by Gender**: Percentage of male and female employees.
- **Employees by Hourly Rate**: Distribution of employees based on their hourly wage.
- **Employees by Job Role**: Number of employees in various job roles such as Sales Executive, Research Scientist, Laboratory Technician, etc.
- **Employees by Education Field**: Distribution of employees across different educational backgrounds like Life Sciences, Medical, Marketing, etc.
- **Employees by Department**: Number of employees in departments such as Research & Development, Sales, and Human Resources.
- **Employees by Marital Status**: Distribution of employees based on their marital status (Divorced, Married, Single).
- **Employees by Business Travel**: Frequency of business travel among employees (Travel Rarely, Travel Frequently, Non-Travel).

## Data Sources
The data used in this dashboard was obtained from Kaggle, providing a reliable and comprehensive dataset for analysis.

## Usage
To use the dashboard:
1. Navigate through the different sections to view the respective data visualizations.
2. Analyze the distributions and percentages to gain insights into the workforce demographics.
3. Use the information for strategic decision-making, workforce planning, and policy formulation.
